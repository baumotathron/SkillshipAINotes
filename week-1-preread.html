üöÄ What Is Product Management?

Product Management is the art and science of solving meaningful problems through products. It sits at the intersection of business, design, and technology.

Product Managers ask three questions every day:

  1. Who is our user?

  3. How will we know if it worked?

A PM‚Äôs daily work includes writing problem statements, defining user journeys, coordinating with designers and developers, and analysing data.
